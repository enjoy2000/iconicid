Representative Director’s message

2008, ICOCNI was established in Ho Chi Minh Viet Nam with the thoughts,“make the foundation for the companies and individuals who challenges beyond the borders to play an active.”The globalization and Indonesia is one of the important country for Japan.

In the other hand of increasing and expanding business, there is a big problem how to manage the employees becoming a 2012 multinational. There are big differences between developing countries in Asia and Japan such as cultural differences, value and lifestyles. And those developing countries in Asia has various countries and region with diverse differences.

As a first step to serve the service which is rooted in various regions, we started taking action by setting up new branch offices in Indonesia from 2012. Indonesia is one of the important developing country in Asia. We assist, by giving solution to the companies and individuals challenging the opportunities here in Indonesia.

We would like to help the companies and the individualsto enhance each other. Then contribute to develop through friendly competition in both countries. This is our thoughts
