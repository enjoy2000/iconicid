Recruiting service

Merit

  • 1. We can assist you to recruit both Japanese and Indonesiaese in Indonesia.
  • 2. Our recruiting consulting fee is charged upon contingent fee basis.
  • 3. We maintain HR stock with various backgrounds lured through various marketing mix.
  • 4. You can streamline relevant administrative works upon recruitment in your company by outsourcing them to ICONIC.

Procedure

  1. Contact ICONIC

    Please contact us for your interest in our recruiting service. Our consultant will return to you soon for detailed job offer infromation.

  2. Confirmation of Job Offer Detailes

    We shall request you to double-check job description and Condition in order to prevent misunderstanding.

  3. Short-List by ICONIC

    ICONIC shall short-list appropriate candidates in line with the job offer provided by you amongst those registered in ICONICBANK(HR database).

  4. Interview

    ICONIC shall arrange interviews between you and the short-listed candidates.

  5. Final Selection

    You'll conduct final selection and inform ICONIC of the result. ICONIC shall then inform the candidates of the reuslt

  6. Confirmation of Condition of Employment

    ICONIC shall confirm the intention of joining your company of the finally selected candidate and negotiate final conditions of employment such as salary, benefits, first working day etc with him/her.

  7. Labor contract

    The labor contract shall be closed between you and the finally selected candidate.

  8. Start working

    ICONIC shall contact you to confirm whether the candidate will have started working without any problem on the first working day.
