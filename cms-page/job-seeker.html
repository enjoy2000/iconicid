For Job Seekers

ICONIC is your best partner to find a job in Indonesia!

ICONIC introduces job opportunities in foreign invested companies (especially Japanese Companies) in Indonesia. Once you register yourself as ICONIC member, you'll keep informed of latest job information in Indonesia. Our job information and career consultation service for Jobseekers are all provided by FREE OF CHARGE!

Free career counseling service

  • We provide free career counseling service for those who are looking for job opportunities in Indonesia. We have not only the job offer information already updated on our website but also undisclosed one with us. Please feel free to contact us if you are interested in finding a job in Indonesia.
Procedure
  1. Contact ICONIC

    Please contact us by registering yourself as Jobseeker or sending your CV by e-mail to us. It is recommended to mention the job offer number in which you are interested when sending e-mail.
    Even if you cannot find any job offer on our website in which you are interested, please don't hesitate to contact us as we usually have many other undisclosed job offers with us.

  2. Reply by ICONIC

    Our consultants will contact you after your registration.

  3. Career Counseling

    We will have career counseling sessions in our Ho Chi Minh City office or Ha Noi office with those who reside in Indonesia or via Skype with those who reside outside of Indonesia.

  4. Interview by Employer

    Basically you'll be expected to have an interview with the employer in Indonesia. Therefore, it is recommended to be ready to fly to Indonesia for interview in case you reside outside of Indonesia. However, some employers may sometimes accept to have interview via Skype or TV phone or in Head Quarter in Japan depending on their corporate policy.

  5. Start working

    We'll inform you of the result of the last selection by the employer and negotiate with you the labor conditions such as first working day, salary, benefit etc on behalf of the employer. Once agreed between both parties, you'll start working for the employer.
